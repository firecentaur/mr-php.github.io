Provide enterprise level ecommerce support and extensions for a company with 8 physical locations across Australia and New Zealand.

  • Created custom admin based on zenCart database allowing extended functionality and ease of development.
  • Implemented multistore functionality to allow independent category, product and price management for each of the 8 store branches including a NZ branch which trades in NZ$.
  • Modified zenCart front end to meet the many advanced needs of the client.
  • Search Engine Optimisation including Advanced SEO URLs, 301 redirects for duplicate content.
